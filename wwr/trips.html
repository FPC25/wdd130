Adventure Levels

Beginners

Perfect for newcomers, our beginner trips feature the scenic Sunset Rapids (6km) and Devil's Canyon rapids (8km). These gentle routes offer a perfect blend of small rapids and calmer waters, allowing you to learn rafting basics while enjoying nature. Starting at $45 per person, these trips are an excellent introduction to the thrill of rafting.

Experienced

Step up your adventure with our experienced-level trips! Los Dientes del Diablo (12km) and Half Day Adventure (15km) offer more challenging rapids and longer routes. These trips, priced from $75, feature technical rapids, stunning wilderness, and the perfect balance of excitement and skill development.

Advanced

The Big Mallard, our premier 20km advanced course, is the ultimate rafting challenge. At $105 per person, this intense journey offers the most thrilling rapids, demanding technical skills, and an unforgettable adventure through spectacular river terrain. Recommended for experienced rafters only.
